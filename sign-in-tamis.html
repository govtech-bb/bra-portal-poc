Sign in to BRA online services

Sign in with your TAMIS account

Enter the Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) and password you use to sign in to TAMIS.

Your TIN is on your tax returns and any letters from BRA. For example, 100-005-8216-487.

I have forgotten my password

Don’t have a TAMIS account? Create new sign in details or apply for a TIN.

Problems signing in?

If you cannot sign in, call BRA on 246-429-2273 between 8:30am and 4:30pm, Monday to Friday.
