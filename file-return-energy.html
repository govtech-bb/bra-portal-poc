Did you invest in renewable energy in 2025?

You may be able to claim a deduction for the cost of installing solar, wind or other renewable energy systems, or for an energy audit on your home or business.

Did you invest in renewable energy or an energy audit for your home?

For example, installing solar panels, a solar water heater, or an electrical retrofit.

Enter the total cost in BDS$. You can claim up to 150% of the cost over the next few years.
