Step 3 of 6 — Your income

Foreign currency earnings allowance Advanced

If part of your business income was earned in a foreign currency, you may be entitled to a rebate under Schedules Four and Five of the Income Tax Act. We’ll do the calculation. You just give us the three numbers below.

When this applies

Only complete this section if your business earned money in a currency other than Barbadian dollars and you are claiming the foreign currency earnings rebate. If you’re not sure, ask your accountant or read the BRA guidance on foreign currency earnings.

What happens next

BRA will use these three numbers to work out your deemed profit, your effective rate, and the rebate you can claim — following Schedules Four and Five of the Income Tax Act. You’ll see the rebate on your Check Your Answers page.
