File your Personal Income Tax return for 2025

Last updated: 4 January 2026

Your return for 2025 is due by 30 April 2026. Filing late may result in a penalty.

Use this service to

Tell BRA about the income you earned in 2025 and claim any allowances you are entitled to.

We’ll work out your tax bill or refund and confirm what you need to do next. Most people who earned only employment income can finish in about 10 minutes.

Who can use this service

You can file online if you:

  • were a resident of Barbados during 2025
  • have a Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN)
  • earned employment income, rental income, freelance income, or interest and dividends

If you are filing for a business or partnership, use TAMIS instead.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your TIN (we’ll show this once you sign in)
  • statements for any other income you earned, like rent or freelance work
  • your bank account details if you are owed a refund

We’ll already have your employment income and Pay As You Earn (PAYE) tax from your employer — you just need to check it.

How long it takes

About 10 minutes for most people.

What happens after you file

  • We’ll send you an email to confirm we have your return.
  • If you owe tax, we’ll show you how to pay before the deadline.
  • If you are owed a refund, we’ll pay it to your bank account.

Other ways to file

In TAMIS

If you need advanced features (for example, filing for a business or amending a previous return), use TAMIS.
