BRA online services: sign in or set up an account

Last updated: 4 May 2026

Contents

  1. Sign in or set up an account
  2. Get help with signing in to BRA online services

Sign in or set up an account

Sign in to your Barbados Revenue Authority (BRA) online account for things like Pay As You Earn (PAYE), Personal Income Tax, Value Added Tax (VAT), Corporation Tax and Property and Land Tax.

If you do not already have sign in details, you’ll be able to create them.

Sign in

Which sign in details to use

There are 3 ways to sign in to BRA online services:

  • TAMIS account — sign in with your Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) and password
  • Trident ID — sign in with your Trident ID and password (this also works across other government services on alpha.gov.bb)
  • National Registration Number (NRN) — sign in with your NRN and password

If you already use BRA online services

Continue to use the sign in details you have. This could be:

  • your existing TAMIS account — even if you have a Trident ID from another government service
  • a Trident ID — for personal tax services

If you have never used BRA online services

Choose ‘Create new sign in details’ when signing in to BRA online services, even if you have a Trident ID from another government service or are signed up for BRA email updates.

The service will guide you to create a TAMIS account or a Trident ID.

What you can do with your BRA online account

You can access different services through:

  • your personal tax account
  • your business tax account
  • tax agent accounts for accountants and advisers

Services you sign in to separately

There’s a different way to sign in for:

  • customs and excise services
  • National Insurance and Pensions (NIS) account
  • TAMIS (advanced filing features)

Get help with signing in

If you cannot sign in, call BRA on 246-429-2273 between 8:30am and 4:30pm, Monday to Friday. Have your TIN ready when you call.

You can also reset your password or request a new TIN online.
